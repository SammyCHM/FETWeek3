A Stone's Throw From DTLA!


Highland Park is a suburb in Los Angeles situated just minutes east of a bustling downtown Los Angeles. The area is roughly about 3.475 mi² and is sandwiched between Glendale, Pasadena and neighboring Eagle Rock. It’s no surprise that being one of the first suburbs in Los Angeles, it is also adjacent to the first freeway ever built not only in the state but in the nation! The Arroyo Parkway, also known as the 110 freeway, began construction in the 1930’s and officially opened in December of 1940. Read More About it!
